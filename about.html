About Me

I am a final-year undergraduate student pursuing a Bachelor of Science (Honors) in Artificial Intelligence at Beijing Normal-Hong Kong Baptist University (BNBU), expected to graduate in July 2026.

My research focuses on applying deep learning to real-world problems, including medical image analysis (OCT, MRI, skin cancer), sentiment analysis, graph neural networks, and brain-computer interfaces.

I have published multiple papers in international conferences (ICAID, NNICE, AIIIP, etc.) and completed internships at Tencent and Zhongke Huayi Technology Co., Ltd.

Recipient of the Curiosity Scholarship for academic excellence.
